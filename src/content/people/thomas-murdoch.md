# About
Thomas Murdoch is an undergraduate student in the Mechanical Engineering department at BYU. His interests revolve around creating human centric robotics applications. In his free time he wishes he had he would continue building a mechatronic bread making machine or his bat customizing robot. He is passionate about solving the right problems and not just solving things the right way. He loves meeting new people. If you are interested in chatting about my research please feel free to reach out. 

# Research
Currently Thomas works with Dr. Brady Moon on building up a VTOL quad plane drone which will be used for scientific areal photography, mapping and testing for control algorithms.
