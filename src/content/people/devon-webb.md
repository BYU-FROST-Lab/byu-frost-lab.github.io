# About
Devon Webb is from American Fork, Utah and is a Graduate student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Brigham Young University. He finished his bachelor’s degree at BYU in April 2020 and joined the FROST Lab in August of 2020. His interests are in developing autonomous underwater vehicles and the autonomous diagnosis and repair of those vehicles. He enjoys 3D printing, baking, hiking, and finding new parks to visit with his wife and daughter.



# Research
Devon’s research involves developing fault tolerant robotic vehicles that are capable of autonomously diagnosing communication faults that occur within the internal communication network of the vehicle and when communicating to other vehicles.
